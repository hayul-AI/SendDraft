Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

TL;DR: We don't store your personal message data. No accounts are needed. We use minimal analytics to keep the site running smoothly.

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

SendDraft is built on the principle of minimal data collection. We believe you shouldn't have to trade your personal information for a well-written message draft.

2. Information You Provide

When you use SendDraft to create a message, the details you type into our forms (names, dates, amounts) are processed locally in your browser or temporarily during the generation process. We do not save, store, or log the content of your drafts.

3. Information We Collect Automatically

Like most websites, we collect limited technical information to ensure our service is working correctly. This includes:

  • Usage Data: Which pages you visit and which tools are most popular.
  • Technical Data: Your browser type, device type, and approximate location (city/country level).
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to maintain site functionality and basic analytics.

4. Third-Party Services

We partner with trusted service providers to host our site and analyze traffic. These partners may collect anonymized data according to their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties.

5. Data Security

While we don't store your drafts, we use industry-standard security measures (like HTTPS) to protect any data that passes through our site. Since we don't require accounts, there are no passwords or sensitive profiles for us to manage.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), you may have rights regarding your data. Since we collect very little data and don't link it to individual identities, we fulfill these rights by design. If you have specific questions, feel free to reach out.
