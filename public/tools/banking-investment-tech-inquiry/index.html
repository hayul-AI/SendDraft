Reporting Investment Technical Issues

When dealing with investment and trading platforms, technical issues can be time-sensitive and stressful. Whether it's an order that didn't execute properly or a display error in your portfolio, providing support teams with clear, factual, and detailed information is the fastest way to get a resolution. These templates help you structure your technical inquiry professionally.

FAQ

What should I do if my trade didn't execute?

Contact support immediately. Use this tool to provide the ticker symbol, order type, and the exact time you attempted the trade. This data helps the technical team trace the order in their logs.

How much detail do I need to provide?

The more detail, the better. Screenshots, error messages, and a step-by-step description of how to reproduce the issue are incredibly valuable for technical support teams.
