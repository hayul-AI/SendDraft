Professional Tip Correction Requests

Accidents happen, especially with modern digital payment systems. If you've mistakenly entered an incorrect tip amount, most businesses are happy to help you correct it. Providing clear details about the transaction and the intended amount is the best way to resolve the issue quickly and professionally.

FAQ

How soon should I request a correction?

It's best to reach out as soon as you notice the error. If the transaction is still pending, the business may be able to adjust it before it finalizes. If it has already cleared, they may need to issue a partial refund.

What if I don't have my receipt?

You can still request a correction by providing the date, total amount, and the last four digits of the card you used. This information allows the business to locate the transaction in their system.
