Tips

Professional Stop Payment Requests

Whether it's a lost check, a billing error, or a change in circumstances, requesting a stop payment requires clear and precise communication. Providing the exact details of the transaction to your bank or payment provider ensures they can identify and block the specific payment before it is finalized.

FAQ

Can all payments be stopped?

Not always. Checks and scheduled ACH transfers can often be stopped if you act before they are processed. However, finalized electronic transfers and debit card transactions are much harder, and sometimes impossible, to stop.

What is the difference between a stop payment and a dispute?

A stop payment is requested *before* a payment is finalized to prevent it from happening. A dispute (or chargeback) is initiated *after* a payment has already been completed to recover the funds.
