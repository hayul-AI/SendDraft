Professional Support Requests

Whether you're reaching out to a major corporation or a local business, your request for support should be clear, professional, and purpose-driven. By articulating the value of your project and the specific type of contribution you're seeking, you make it easier for potential supporters to say yes.

FAQ

Should I include a specific amount?

It's often helpful to provide a suggested amount or a range, as this gives the donor a baseline. However, for general support requests, you can leave it open-ended to allow for flexibility.

What kind of recognition is standard?

Common recognition includes logo placement on promotional materials, mentions on social media or websites, and verbal acknowledgment during events. Tailor your offer to the level of support requested.
