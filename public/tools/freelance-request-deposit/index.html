FAQ

How do I use this request deposit message?

Simply fill in the required fields in the form above, select your preferred tone (Gentle, Neutral, or Firm), and click 'Generate'. You can then copy the draft and paste it into your messaging app.

Can I customize the generated message?

Yes! Once you copy the text, you can paste it into your preferred messaging platform and make any final adjustments or additions to fit your specific needs.

Which tone is right for me?

Use **Gentle** for first-time requests or when maintaining a friendly relationship is a priority. Use **Neutral** for standard business matters. Use **Firm** for urgent issues or when previous requests have been ignored.

Absolutely. SendDraft is a static tool, meaning everything happens locally in your browser. We do not store or see any of the information you enter into the form.
