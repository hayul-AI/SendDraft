Price Adjustment Basics

Many retailers offer price protection or price adjustment policies. If you purchase an item and notice the price has dropped shortly after, you can often request a refund for the difference. Writing a clear, professional request is the first step toward getting your money back.

FAQ

What is a typical price adjustment window?

While policies vary, most major retailers offer price adjustments within 7 to 14 days of the original purchase. Some premium credit cards also offer price protection as a benefit.

Do I need to provide proof of the lower price?

Yes, providing a link to the product page showing the current lower price or a photo of the store tag is usually required for support to process your request.
