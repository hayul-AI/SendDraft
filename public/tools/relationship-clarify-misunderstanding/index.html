Tips for Writing a Clear Message

  • Be Concise: Stick to the facts and keep your sentences short to ensure the recipient understands your point quickly.
  • Include Identifiers: Always provide relevant numbers like order IDs, account IDs, or dates to help the recipient take action.
  • State Your Goal: Clearly explain what you want the recipient to do next (e.g., "I am requesting a refund").
  • Proofread: Even though the generator produces a draft, always review it to ensure it perfectly fits your specific context.
