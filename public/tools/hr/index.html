Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use these templates?

Pick a tool, fill in the fields, choose your tone, and click "Generate". Then copy and paste the result into your messaging app!

Which tone should I choose?

Gentle is for first contact or maintaining a friendly relationship. Neutral is for standard business matters. Firm is for urgent requests or when previous attempts were ignored.

Is my data stored on your servers?

No. Everything happens client-side in your browser. We don't store or see your private details or generated messages.

Are these templates legal advice?

No. These are for general communication only. For serious legal, financial, or HR matters, please consult a professional.
