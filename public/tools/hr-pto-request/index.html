Professional Time Off Requests

A well-structured time off request helps your manager and team prepare for your absence. By providing clear dates, times, and a coverage plan, you demonstrate professionalism and commitment to your role even while you're away. These templates help you communicate your request effectively, whether you're taking a long-awaited vacation or need a few hours for a personal matter.

FAQ

How much notice should I give for a time off request?

The standard notice period varies by company, but typically two weeks for a short vacation and more for longer leaves is recommended. For medical appointments, give as much notice as possible.

What should I include in my coverage plan?

Mention specific colleagues who have agreed to handle your urgent tasks, or provide instructions on where to find necessary information while you're out.
