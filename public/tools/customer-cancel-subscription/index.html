Professional Cancellation Templates

Requesting a subscription cancellation should be straightforward. Whether you're moving on from a software service, a gym membership, or a monthly box, clarity is essential to ensure your request is processed without delay.

FAQ

Which tone should I use?

Use **Gentle** if you enjoyed the service but no longer need it. Use **Neutral** for standard business cancellations. Use **Firm** if you've had issues or want to ensure renewal is stopped immediately without further interaction.

Do I need to give a reason?

No, you are generally not required to provide a reason, but it can be helpful for the company to improve their service. Keep it short and factual.
